Our Mission

We are here for you. Our intent is to provide you with a starting point and that little push you may need to put into action your ideas for a stronger, fitter, healthier you. We have the experience, knowledge and practice of matching the combination of exercise/activity, nutrition, and daily habits that best fit your personal interests, general health, and goals. The idea is to incorporate the personal assessment and suggestions into your lifestyle. You can work up to a 10 mile hike, or an hour of pool laps and still enjoy your cookies, beer and pizza. It's not about sacrifice but enhancing the way you spend your time and treat your body.

The Team

Our staff is a broad mix of personal trainers, nutritionists, sports doctors, physical therapists and like-minded staff who balance the physical assessment, and personlized recommendations with the administrative requirements.You will receive information and tips on how to begin a new activity, build strength, avoid injury, treat soreness, as well as the where, and when you can try new sports.
